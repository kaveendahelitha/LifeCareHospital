About LifeCare Hospitals

Life care hospital is a multi-super specialty hospital with high technology diagnostic and curative facilities. It offers advanced medical technology and expert medical care, thus eliminating the need for people to travel out of Sri Lanka for specialized medical treatment.
The launch of the hospital and the overwhelming response it received from the people demonstrated a long felt need for superior healthcare in a pleasant environment.
Driven by the Vision to be the Hospital of Tomorrow, the medical institution has come to be known as a centre of excellence and a preferred healthcare institution in the country.

Mission, Vision and Values

Our Mission

To improve the health of our community and the world by setting the standard of excellence in patient care.

Our Vission

To lead the world in the diagnosis and treatment of disease and to train tomorrow's great physicans, nurses and scientists.
